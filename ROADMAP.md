# WoW LK Custom Server — Roadmap

## Guiding rule
If a feature threatens stability or performance, it is redesigned or delayed.

---

## Patch 1 — Mercenary System (Signature Mechanic)

**Goal:**  
Introduce a permanent gameplay mechanic that enables solo and small-group play,
independent of online population.

**Key points:**
- Always available system
- Does NOT scale with online players
- Separate from population bots
- First core identity feature of the server

---

## Patch 2 — Level 90 + Crafting System

**Goal:**  
Extend character progression and introduce a long-term crafting loop
that integrates with mercenaries and future content.

---

## Patch 3 — New Zone + Questlines

**Goal:**  
Add narrative and exploration content designed for solo and mercenary-assisted play.

---

## Patch 4 — Custom Battleground Format

**Goal:**  
Introduce a unique PvP experience that can function independently of high population.

---

## Beyond
Future patches are selected based on:
- player engagement
- system health
- performance data
- community feedback
